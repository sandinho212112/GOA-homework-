BMW

Introduction to BMW

BMW is a German multinational company which produces luxury vehicles and motorcycles.

History of BMW

The company was founded in 1916 as a manufacturer of aircraft engines, which it produced from 1917 until 1918 and again from 1933 to 1945.

BMW Headquarters

BMW's headquarters are located in Munich, Bavaria.

BMW's Market Position

BMW is one of the best-selling luxury automakers in the world.
